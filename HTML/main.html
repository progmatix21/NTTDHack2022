Health Predictor is a web application that uses the power of Machine Learning to predict a cardiovascular or diabetic condition based on health parameters entered by the user. Follow these instructions to use this app.

  1. Select type of disease in Navigation bar.
  2. Follow instructions to gather relevant data about your condition.
  3. Enter the health parameters as requested in the form.
  4. Press submit after entering details.
  5. Read the result on the response page.
